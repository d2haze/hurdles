300H → 400H Conversion
Estimate an athlete's 400H potential from their 300H time using the Ross & Hernandez de Ross fatigue and distance ratio model. The 400H covers 100 more meters with 2 additional hurdles — conditioning and race fitness are the primary variable beyond raw speed.
400H ≈ (300H × 1.333) + Fatigue Factor
Distance ratio method · Fatigue factor accounts for lactic accumulation over the additional 100m · Range: +3.5 to +5.5 sec depending on conditioning level
